Brooklyn 99

Brooklyn 99 is a procedural comedy show that follows NYPD Detectives in Brooklyn's 99th Precinct.


The show follows Detective Jake Peralta played by Andy Samberg, and his colleagues in their day-to days in the police precinct.
It casts Andy Samberg as Jake Peralta, Melissa Fumero as Detective mega nerd Amy Santiago, Stephanie Beatriz as the silent but strong Rosa Diaz, Chelsea Peretti as asisstant Gina Linetti, Terry Crews as
Sergeant Terry Jeffords, Joe Lo Truglio as hardworking Charles Boyle, Dirk Blocker as old Desk Jockey Hitchcok, Joel Miller as old desk jockey number 2 Norm Scully, and Andre Braugher as Serious Captain Holt.
